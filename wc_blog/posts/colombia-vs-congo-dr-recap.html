Colombia Edges Congo DR 1-0 in World Cup 2026

Tuesday, June 23, 2026

Full-time result

Colombia 1 — 0 Congo DR

Before kick-off: read our preview of Colombia vs Congo DR — we predicted 2-0.

We predicted a 2-0 win for Colombia, but it finished 1-0 — a narrower margin than expected, yet the outcome was the same. Colombia's victory over Congo DR was a tightly contested affair, with the South Americans showcasing their superior quality to take all three points in their Group K clash.

Colombia's Winning Formula

Colombia's performance was characterized by their disciplined defense and swift counter-attacks. Their goal, scored in the 65th minute, was a testament to their ability to capitalize on scoring opportunities. The Colombian team's midfield duo controlled the tempo of the game, often leaving Congo DR chasing shadows as they struggled to maintain possession.

Congo DR's Valiant Effort

Congo DR, despite the loss, demonstrated remarkable resilience and determination. Their defense, although eventually breached, was resolute for much of the match, and they created several half-chances that could have altered the outcome. However, their inability to convert these opportunities into concrete goals ultimately cost them the game.

Why It Went This Way

The decisive factor in Colombia's win was their tactical flexibility and ability to adapt to Congo DR's defensive approach. By switching between a 4-2-3-1 and a 4-3-3 formation, Colombia managed to find spaces and exploit weaknesses in the Congo DR defense. This adaptability, combined with their superior technical skill, allowed Colombia to dictate the flow of the game and ultimately secure the victory.

This result has significant implications for Group K, as Colombia now finds themselves in a strong position to advance to the next round. Congo DR, on the other hand, faces an uphill battle to qualify, but their performance against Colombia suggests they still have the potential to cause an upset in their remaining matches.
