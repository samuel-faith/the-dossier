Paraguay Stun Turkey with 1-0 Win

Friday, June 19, 2026

Full-time result

Turkey 0 — 1 Paraguay

Before kick-off: read our preview of Turkey vs Paraguay — we predicted 2-0.

We predicted a comfortable 2-0 win for Turkey, but the reality was far from it. Instead, Paraguay pulled off a stunning 1-0 victory, leaving our pre-match forecast well off the mark. The result was a testament to Paraguay's resilience and Turkey's inability to capitalize on their chances.

Paraguay's Winning Formula

Paraguay's success can be attributed to their well-organized defense and clever counter-attacks. They soaked up Turkey's pressure, often committing bodies to block shots and intercept passes. When they won the ball back, they transitioned quickly, catching Turkey off guard with swift breaks. This approach paid dividends, as they managed to find the net despite being outshot by their opponents.

Turkey's Frustrating Evening

Turkey, on the other hand, will be left scratching their heads, wondering how they failed to score. They dominated possession and created several promising opportunities, but a combination of poor finishing and Paraguay's dogged defense denied them. Turkey's attacking play often lacked a cutting edge, with their final ball frequently letting them down. As the match wore on, their frustration grew, and they began to take more risks, which Paraguay were happy to exploit on the counter.

Why It Went This Way

The turning point in the match came when Paraguay's defense held firm against a sustained period of Turkish pressure. As Turkey committed more players forward, Paraguay sensed an opportunity to catch them on the break. A swift counter-attack ensued, and Paraguay's striker made no mistake, slotting the ball past the Turkish goalkeeper. This goal gave Paraguay the confidence to see out the match, and they did so with relative comfort. Turkey's inability to adapt to Paraguay's defensive setup and their own lack of clinical finishing ultimately cost them the match.

The implications of this result are significant for Group D. Paraguay's win gives them a vital three points, while Turkey's defeat leaves them with work to do to get back on track. As the group stages unfold, this match could prove to be a pivotal moment in the tournament. For now, Paraguay will bask in the glory of their hard-fought victory, while Turkey will look to regroup and refocus ahead of their next challenge.
