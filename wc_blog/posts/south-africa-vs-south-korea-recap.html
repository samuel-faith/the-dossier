South Africa Edge Past South Korea 1-0

Wednesday, June 24, 2026

Full-time result

South Africa 1 — 0 South Korea

Before kick-off: read our preview of South Africa vs South Korea — we predicted 2-0.

We predicted a 2-0 win for South Africa, but it was a single goal that made the difference as they edged past South Korea 1-0 in their World Cup Group A clash. While we were close in our prediction, the actual match was a testament to the resilience and defensive prowess of both teams, with South Africa's single goal proving to be the decisive factor.

South Africa's Winning Formula

South Africa's victory was built on a solid defensive foundation, with their backline absorbing pressure and limiting South Korea's attacking opportunities. Their midfield also played a crucial role, controlling the tempo of the game and creating chances through swift counter-attacks. The goal, when it came, was a result of this persistence and pressure, showcasing South Africa's ability to capitalize on scoring opportunities.

South Korea's Unrewarded Efforts

Despite the loss, South Korea can take heart from their performance, which saw them create several promising attacking moves. However, their inability to convert these chances into goals ultimately cost them the match. Their defense, too, was largely solid, but the single lapse in concentration that led to South Africa's goal was enough to decide the outcome. South Korea will need to work on their finishing if they are to make an impact in the rest of the tournament.

Why It Went This Way

Tactically, the match was decided by South Africa's ability to adapt and exploit the spaces left by South Korea's aggressive pressing. By sitting deep and inviting pressure, South Africa were able to catch their opponents off guard with quick transitions, one of which led to the decisive goal. This approach also allowed them to neutralize South Korea's attacking threat, limiting their opponents to few clear-cut chances and ensuring that their defense was not overly stretched.

The outcome of this match has significant implications for the group standings, with South Africa now in a strong position to advance to the next round. For South Korea, the task ahead is more daunting, but not impossible. They will need to regroup and refocus for their upcoming matches, aiming to secure the points necessary to stay in contention. As the tournament progresses, the intensity and stakes will only increase, making every match a crucial battle for survival and advancement.
