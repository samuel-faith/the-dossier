Uruguay and Cape Verde Islands Share Spoils in 2-2 Thriller

Sunday, June 21, 2026

Full-time result

Before kick-off: read our preview of Uruguay vs Cape Verde Islands — we predicted 2-0.

We predicted a comfortable 2-0 win for Uruguay, but the reality was far more dramatic — the South Americans were held to a 2-2 draw by a spirited Cape Verde Islands side. It was a result that will leave Uruguay wondering what could have been, while Cape Verde Islands will be buoyed by their impressive performance against a team expected to challenge for a spot in the knockout stages.

Uruguay's Missed Opportunity

Uruguay started the match strongly, with their experienced attack causing problems for the Cape Verde Islands defense. They took the lead through a well-worked goal, but failed to build on their advantage. Instead, they allowed Cape Verde Islands back into the game, and the underdogs capitalized on their chances to level the score. Uruguay's inability to see out the win will be a concern for their coach, who will know that his team needs to be more clinical in front of goal if they are to progress in the tournament.

Cape Verde Islands' Brave Performance

Cape Verde Islands, on the other hand, can take great pride in their performance. They were written off by many before the match, but they showed that they are a team to be reckoned with. Their pace and agility caused problems for the Uruguay defense, and they took their chances well to earn a deserved draw. The result will give them hope that they can cause an upset in their remaining group matches and potentially sneak into the knockout stages.

Why It Went This Way

The key to Cape Verde Islands' success was their ability to exploit the spaces left by Uruguay's attacking full-backs. Time and again, they found themselves with room to run at the Uruguay defense, and they made the most of their opportunities. Uruguay, on the other hand, will rue their missed chances and their failure to adapt to the Cape Verde Islands' tactics. The draw was a fair result, but Uruguay will know that they should have won the game.

The result leaves both teams with work to do in Group H. Uruguay will need to regroup and refocus if they are to achieve their goals in the tournament, while Cape Verde Islands will look to build on their impressive performance and cause more upsets in the group stage. One thing is certain — this match will be remembered as one of the most exciting of the tournament so far, and both teams will take credit for their part in it.
