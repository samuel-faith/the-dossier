Brazil Thrash Scotland 3-0 in World Cup 2026

Wednesday, June 24, 2026

Full-time result

Scotland 0 — 3 Brazil

Before kick-off: read our preview of Scotland vs Brazil — we predicted 2-0.

We predicted a 2-0 win for Brazil, but they had other plans, thrashing Scotland 3-0 in their Group C clash. While we were right about the outcome, the scale of the victory was more convincing than anticipated. Brazil's performance was a testament to their strength and depth, leaving Scotland with a tough mountain to climb in their quest to advance from the group.

Brazil's Dominant Display

Brazil were in control from the outset, with their attacking quartet of Richarlison, Vinicius Jr, Neymar, and Raphinha causing chaos for the Scottish defense. The movement and interplay between the four was a joy to watch, and they consistently found space to exploit behind the Scottish backline. The goals, when they came, were a result of this pressure, with Brazil's attackers showing their clinical finishing ability.

Scotland's Struggles

Scotland, on the other hand, struggled to cope with Brazil's attacking prowess. Their defense, which had been solid in their opening match, was torn apart by the Brazilian forwards. The Scottish midfield, usually so robust, was bypassed with ease, and their attackers were starved of service. It was a tough evening for Steve Clarke's side, who will need to regroup and refocus ahead of their next match.

Why It Went This Way

Tactically, Brazil's victory was a result of their ability to stretch Scotland's defense with their wide attackers. Vinicius Jr and Raphinha consistently hugged the touchline, pulling the Scottish full-backs out of position and creating space for Neymar and Richarlison to operate in. This movement and flexibility caused Scotland's defense to become disjointed, and they were unable to cope with the subsequent attacks. Brazil's coach, Tite, will be pleased with the way his team executed their game plan, and they will be confident of advancing from the group.

The result leaves Scotland with a mountain to climb if they are to advance from Group C. They will need to win their remaining matches and hope that other results go in their favor. Brazil, on the other hand, are well-placed to top the group, and will be looking to build on this impressive performance in their next match. With this win, Brazil have sent a statement to the rest of the teams in the tournament, and they will be one of the teams to watch in the knockout stages.
