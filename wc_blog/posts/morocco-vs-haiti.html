Morocco Looks to Seal Group C Dominance Against Haiti

Wednesday, June 24, 2026

With two games played, Morocco has established itself as a force to be reckoned with in Group C, following a hard-fought draw against Brazil and a narrow win over Scotland. As they prepare to face Haiti, they'll be looking to build on this momentum and cement their position in the group.

Morocco's Rising Confidence

Morocco's performances so far have been characterized by their resilience and ability to adapt to different opponents. Their draw against Brazil, in particular, was a testament to their defensive solidity, while the win over Scotland showcased their capacity to grind out results when needed. With this newfound confidence, they'll be eager to exploit Haiti's vulnerabilities and secure a crucial three points.

Haiti's Uphill Battle

Haiti, on the other hand, has struggled to make an impact in the group, suffering consecutive defeats at the hands of Scotland and Brazil. Despite these setbacks, they'll be determined to prove themselves against Morocco and potentially cause an upset. However, with their attacking woes and defensive concerns, it's an uphill battle for the Haitian side.

The Tactical Battle to Watch

The midfield area will be crucial in this encounter, as Morocco's creative forces look to break down Haiti's defensive resistance. The Haitian side will need to find a way to nullify Morocco's attacking threats while also creating scoring opportunities of their own. This tactical battle will likely decide the outcome of the match, with the team that gains control in midfield ultimately emerging victorious.

Given Morocco's current form and Haiti's struggles, it's likely that the North African side will come out on top, potentially by a two-goal margin, as they look to assert their dominance in Group C.
