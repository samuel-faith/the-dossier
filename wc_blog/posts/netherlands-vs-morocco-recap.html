Morocco Stun Netherlands with 4-3 Thriller

Monday, June 29, 2026

Full-time result

Netherlands 3 — 4 Morocco

In a stunning turn of events, Morocco pulled off a thrilling 4-3 victory over the Netherlands, leaving the Group Stage of the FIFA World Cup 2026 wide open. The match, played on a sweltering Monday evening, saw both teams create numerous scoring opportunities, but it was Morocco's clinical finishing that ultimately decided the outcome. The Netherlands, known for their disciplined defensive structure, found themselves on the back foot for much of the game, struggling to contain Morocco's pacey attacking trio.

Morocco's Attacking Masterclass

Morocco's victory was built on the back of a blistering attacking performance, with their front three of Soussi, Aboukhlal, and En-Nesyri causing chaos in the Dutch defense. The Moroccan midfield, marshaled by the imperious Amrabat, controlled the tempo of the game, allowing their attackers to make runs behind the Dutch defense. From the clever through-balls to the precision finishing, Morocco's attack was a joy to behold, leaving the Netherlands reeling in their wake.

The Netherlands, on the other hand, will be left to ponder their defensive shortcomings, which ultimately cost them the game. Despite creating numerous scoring opportunities, the Dutch were unable to capitalize on their chances, with their finishing often lacking the precision and composure displayed by their Moroccan counterparts. The Dutch defense, usually a bastion of solidity, was breached time and again by Morocco's attackers, with the Netherlands' full-backs struggling to cope with the pace and trickery of Morocco's wingers. As the game wore on, the Dutch became increasingly desperate, committing more and more players forward in search of an equalizer, but Morocco's defense held firm, securing a famous victory for the North African nation.

Why It Went This Way

Tactically, the game was won and lost in the midfield battle, where Morocco's Amrabat and his colleagues outmuscled and outmaneuvered their Dutch counterparts. The Moroccan midfield's ability to control the tempo of the game allowed their attackers to make runs behind the Dutch defense, catching them off guard time and again. The Netherlands, meanwhile, struggled to adapt to Morocco's high-pressing game, often finding themselves pinned back by the Moroccan attackers. As the game wore on, the Dutch became increasingly stretched, with their defense eventually succumbing to the pressure. In the end, it was Morocco's clever tactics and clinical finishing that proved the difference, securing a famous victory for the underdogs.

The result throws the Group Stage wide open, with all teams still in with a chance of progressing to the knockout stages. For Morocco, the victory is a huge confidence booster, while the Netherlands will need to regroup and reassess their tactics ahead of their next fixture. One thing is certain, however – this match will be remembered as one of the most thrilling encounters of the tournament so far, with Morocco's stunning victory over the Netherlands an early contender for match of the tournament.
