Algeria Edge Jordan 2-1 in Group J Thriller

Monday, June 22, 2026

Full-time result

Jordan 1 — 2 Algeria

Before kick-off: read our preview of Jordan vs Algeria — we predicted 2-0.

Our prediction of a 2-0 Algeria win was close, but not quite on the mark, as Jordan pushed their opponents all the way in a 2-1 defeat. The match, played out on a hot Monday evening, saw Algeria's class ultimately tell, but Jordan will take heart from a spirited performance that suggested they are more than capable of causing problems in this group.

Algeria's Winning Formula

Algeria's victory was built on a strong defensive foundation, with their backline holding firm in the face of some intense Jordanian pressure. They were happy to soak up the pressure and hit Jordan on the break, using their pacey wingers to devastating effect. The Algerian midfield, marshaled by their experienced captain, controlled the tempo of the game, dictating when to push forward and when to sit back and absorb pressure.

Jordan's Gallant Effort

Jordan, despite the defeat, can take pride in their performance. They created several clear-cut chances, particularly in the first half, and will be disappointed not to have taken more from the game. Their front line was lively and caused problems for the Algerian defense, but ultimately, they were unable to find the breakthrough they needed to win the game. Jordan's midfield worked tirelessly to try and gain a foothold in the game, but Algeria's experience and quality told in the end.

Why It Went This Way

The key to Algeria's win was their ability to adapt to Jordan's high-pressing game. By sitting deep and using their pace on the break, they were able to catch Jordan off guard and create several good chances. Jordan, on the other hand, struggled to break down Algeria's well-organized defense, and their lack of composure in front of goal ultimately cost them the game. The introduction of Algeria's substitutes also had a significant impact, as they added fresh legs and a new dimension to their attack.

The result leaves Algeria in a strong position in Group J, but Jordan will know that they are still very much in the mix. With several tough games still to come, both teams will need to be at their best if they are to progress to the next round. For now, Algeria will celebrate a hard-fought win, while Jordan will regroup and look to bounce back in their next match.
