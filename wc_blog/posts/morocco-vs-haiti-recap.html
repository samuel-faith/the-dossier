Morocco Edges Haiti 4-2 in High-Scoring Affair

Wednesday, June 24, 2026

Full-time result

Morocco 4 — 2 Haiti

Before kick-off: read our preview of Morocco vs Haiti — we predicted 2-0.

We predicted a comfortable 2-0 win for Morocco, but it turned out to be a far more entertaining and high-scoring match than expected, with the North Africans ultimately emerging 4-2 victors over Haiti. While our prediction was off the mark in terms of scoreline, Morocco's victory was always the likely outcome, given their superior squad depth and experience.

Morocco's Attacking Prowess

Morocco's attack was in fine form, with their forwards causing problems for the Haitian defense all game long. Their pace and movement were too much for Haiti to handle, and they capitalized on their chances to score four goals. Morocco's midfield also played a key role, controlling the tempo of the game and creating scoring opportunities with their passing and vision.

Haiti's Courageous Effort

Despite the loss, Haiti can take pride in their performance, as they refused to give up and scored two goals of their own. Their counter-attacks were a threat to Morocco throughout the match, and they will be disappointed not to have taken more from the game. Haiti's squad lacks the depth and experience of Morocco, but they made up for it with their determination and spirit.

Why It Went This Way

The key to Morocco's victory was their ability to exploit the spaces behind Haiti's defense. Their forwards made intelligent runs, and their midfielders picked out passes that put the Haitian backline under pressure. Haiti, on the other hand, struggled to cope with Morocco's pace and movement, and their defense was often caught out of position. The match was an open and entertaining one, with both teams creating chances and playing their part in an exciting spectacle.

The result leaves Morocco in a strong position in Group C, while Haiti will need to regroup and focus on their upcoming matches if they are to have any chance of advancing. Morocco's performance will give them confidence, but they will also be aware that they need to tighten up their defense if they are to make a deep run in the tournament.
