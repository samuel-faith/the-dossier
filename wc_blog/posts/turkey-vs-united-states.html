Turkey Seek Revival Against In-Form United States

Thursday, June 25, 2026

With two defeats from their opening matches, Turkey's World Cup campaign is on the brink. They face an uphill task against a confident United States side, who have impressed with back-to-back wins in Group D.

United States on a Roll

The United States have been one of the standout teams in the tournament so far, with convincing victories over Paraguay and Australia. Their attacking flair and solid defensive shape have made them a formidable opponent, and they will be looking to maintain their momentum against Turkey.

Turkey's Last-Ditch Effort

Turkey, on the other hand, have struggled to find their footing in the tournament. After narrow defeats to Australia and Paraguay, they need a win to keep their hopes alive. They will need to dig deep and find a way to break down the United States' defense if they are to stand a chance.

The Tactical Battle to Watch

The battle in midfield will be crucial, as the United States look to control the tempo of the game with their possession-based football. Turkey will need to find a way to counter this, potentially by using their pace on the counter-attack to catch the United States off guard.

Given the United States' impressive form and Turkey's struggles, it's likely that the Americans will come out on top, potentially with a 2-0 win.
