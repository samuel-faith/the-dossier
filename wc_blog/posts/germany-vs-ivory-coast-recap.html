Germany Edge Ivory Coast 2-1 in Group E Thriller

Saturday, June 20, 2026

Full-time result

Germany 2 — 1 Ivory Coast

Before kick-off: read our preview of Germany vs Ivory Coast — we predicted 2-0.

We predicted a comfortable 2-0 win for Germany, but it ended up being a nail-biting 2-1 victory over Ivory Coast. The result was closer than anticipated, but the outcome was still in Germany's favour, as they took all three points in this Group E clash.

Germany's Winning Formula

Germany's performance was marked by their characteristic discipline and organisation. They controlled the tempo of the game, creating scoring opportunities through their swift counter-attacks and precise passing. Their two goals were a testament to their clinical finishing, and they could have had more if not for some impressive goalkeeping from Ivory Coast's shot-stopper.

Ivory Coast's Valiant Effort

Ivory Coast, on the other hand, showed glimpses of their attacking potential, but ultimately fell short. They struggled to break down Germany's solid defence, and their lone goal was a consolation prize in the end. Despite the loss, Ivory Coast can take heart from their performance, as they showed they can compete with the tournament's top teams.

Why It Went This Way

The difference maker in this match was Germany's ability to exploit Ivory Coast's defensive vulnerabilities. Germany's coach made some astute tactical decisions, including the introduction of fresh legs in the second half, which helped to stretch Ivory Coast's defence and create scoring opportunities. Ivory Coast, on the other hand, struggled to adapt to Germany's high press, which limited their ability to launch effective counter-attacks.

The win puts Germany in a strong position in Group E, and they will look to build on this momentum in their upcoming matches. Ivory Coast, meanwhile, will need to regroup and refocus if they are to stay in contention for a spot in the knockout stages. With the group still wide open, the next set of fixtures promises to be just as thrilling as this one.
