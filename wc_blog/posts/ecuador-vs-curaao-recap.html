Ecuador and Curaçao Play Out Goalless Draw

Saturday, June 20, 2026

Full-time result

Ecuador 0 — 0 Curaçao

Before kick-off: read our preview of Ecuador vs Curaçao — we predicted 2-0.

We predicted a comfortable 2-0 win for Ecuador, but the reality was far from it as they struggled to break down a resolute Curaçao side, ultimately playing out a 0-0 draw. This result will be seen as a surprise, given the difference in FIFA rankings between the two teams, but Curaçao's defensive solidity and Ecuador's inability to create clear-cut chances meant the match ended without a goal.

Ecuador's Frustrating Evening

Ecuador dominated possession and had numerous half-chances, but their finishing was wayward and their final ball often lacking in quality. Their attacking trio, which had been expected to cause Curaçao problems, was well marshaled by the Curaçao defense, and they struggled to find space or create opportunities. Ecuador's coach will be disappointed with the lack of creativity and incisiveness shown by his team, particularly in the final third.

Curaçao's Defensive Masterclass

Curaçao, on the other hand, can take great pride in their defensive performance. They were well organized, disciplined, and determined, and they made it extremely difficult for Ecuador to find a way through. Their goalkeeper was called into action on a few occasions, but he was equal to everything that came his way, and his defenders in front of him were superb. Curaçao's approach was not overly adventurous, but they deserved their point for their defensive resilience alone.

Why It Went This Way

The main reason for this result was Curaçao's clever tactical approach, which nullified Ecuador's attacking threat and prevented them from playing with their usual fluency. Curaçao's coach got his team selection and tactics spot on, and his players executed the game plan to perfection. Ecuador, on the other hand, will be concerned about their lack of creativity and their inability to adapt to Curaçao's defensive setup. This was a tactical battle that Curaçao won, and they fully deserved their point.

This result leaves Group E wide open, with all teams still having a chance to qualify for the next round. Ecuador will be looking to bounce back from this disappointment, while Curaçao will be buoyed by their impressive performance. The remaining matches in the group promise to be fascinating, and this result has added an extra layer of complexity to the standings.
