Netherlands Look to Extend Winning Streak Against Struggling Tunisia

Thursday, June 25, 2026

The Netherlands will be looking to build on their impressive 5-1 win over Sweden when they face Tunisia in their next Group F fixture. Tunisia, on the other hand, are still searching for their first points of the tournament after consecutive losses to Sweden and Japan.

Netherlands' Momentum

The Netherlands have shown a strong attacking flair in their opening two matches, with their draw against Japan and convincing win over Sweden demonstrating their ability to create scoring opportunities. Their squad depth and tactical flexibility will be key in breaking down Tunisia's defence.

Tunisia's Uphill Battle

Tunisia have struggled to find their footing in the tournament, conceding nine goals in their opening two matches. They will need to significantly improve their defensive solidity if they are to have any chance of taking points from this match. However, their attacking play has shown glimpses of promise, and they may look to exploit any complacency in the Netherlands' backline.

The Tactical Battle to Watch

The battle between the Netherlands' attacking midfielders and Tunisia's defence will be crucial in determining the outcome of this match. If the Netherlands can find space to operate behind Tunisia's midfield, they are likely to create a plethora of scoring opportunities. Conversely, if Tunisia can compact their defence and quickly transition into counter-attacks, they may be able to catch the Netherlands off guard.

Given the Netherlands' superior form and attacking prowess, they are likely to emerge victorious from this encounter, potentially by a comfortable margin.
