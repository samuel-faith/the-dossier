Morocco Edges Scotland 1-0 in World Cup Group C

Friday, June 19, 2026

Full-time result

Scotland 0 — 1 Morocco

Before kick-off: read our preview of Scotland vs Morocco — we predicted 2-0.

We tipped Scotland to dominate Morocco with a 2-0 victory, but the actual outcome was a far more defensive affair, with Morocco edging the match 1-0. The result is a significant upset, given the pre-match expectations, and it raises the stakes for Scotland's remaining group fixtures.

Morocco's Winning Performance

Morocco's victory was built on a solid defensive foundation, with their backline holding firm against Scotland's attacking forays. The winning goal, scored in the second half, was a testament to Morocco's clever use of space and their ability to catch Scotland on the counter. Their midfield controlled the tempo of the game, often dictating the terms of engagement and limiting Scotland's opportunities to create meaningful chances.

Scotland's Disappointing Display

Scotland, on the other hand, struggled to find their rhythm, with their usual attacking flair muted by Morocco's disciplined defense. They created a few half-chances, but these were often snuffed out by Morocco's well-organized rearguard. The Scots' inability to break down Morocco's defense was a major factor in their defeat, and they will need to regroup and reassess their strategy for their next match.

Why It Went This Way

Tactically, Morocco's approach was spot on. They recognized Scotland's attacking strengths and set up to nullify them, using a deep-lying midfield to protect their defense. This allowed them to absorb Scotland's pressure and launch swift counter-attacks, which ultimately led to the winning goal. Morocco's adaptability and willingness to adjust their shape to suit the situation were key factors in their success.

The implications of this result are significant for both teams. Morocco's win gives them a valuable three points in Group C, while Scotland's defeat leaves them with work to do to get back on track. As the group stages unfold, this match could prove to be a pivotal moment in the tournament, and both teams will be keenly aware of the challenges that lie ahead.
